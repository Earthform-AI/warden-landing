## What We Do

We're a research lab building systems grounded in the algebraic structure of the Fano plane PG(2,2), its automorphism group PSL(2,7), and the octonion algebra it encodes. Our work spans protein analysis, custom hardware, systems programming, music theory, and error correction — all connected by shared mathematical foundations.

We believe in open research: numbered experiments, published benchmarks, and code you can run.

## 🔬 Research Threads

Four interconnected lines of inquiry emerging from the Fano plane and its 168 automorphisms:

### Composable Non-Associative Algebra
Making octonions composable through decision-boundary transforms. ComponentTransform group, 8-layer algebraic architecture, cross-domain problem solving.

### Spectral Protein Analysis
Unsupervised protein decomposition via graph Laplacian spectroscopy. The [IBP-ENM](https://github.com/Earthform-AI/ibp-enm) method achieves 100% archetype classification accuracy on our 12-protein benchmark with zero training data, zero homology templates, and zero MD simulations.

### Fano-Structured Music
7 points ↔ 7 notes, 168 automorphisms ↔ 168 harmonic transforms. ChordSpeak maps path history into sound — including an acoustic file transfer protocol.

### Algebraic Video Repair
Corruption as misapplied automorphism → repair as inverse group search. PSL(2,7) automorphism search, spatial algebra via gradient-Fano mapping, belief algebra for error disambiguation.

## 🧠 EarthformOS & Hardware

We're designing a vertical stack from custom hardware up through a kernel to a domain-specific language:

**Hardware → PUC (Parallel Universe Computer)**
A Fano-plane-based evolutionary processor on Lattice ECP5-85F FPGA (ULX3S). Maintains 7 parallel "universes" that evolve toward fitness targets using single-cycle Fano multiplication, cross-diffusion, entropy tracking, and consensus voting.

**Kernel → EarthformOS**
Schedules intent-driven evolutionary processes on PUC hardware. Processes declare fitness targets, evolve state, and beacon for help when local computation is insufficient. Priority is derived from algebraic coherence — how strongly a process's intent activates Fano-plane structure.

**Language → Neural Assembly Language (NAL)**
A systems programming language whose abstractions map directly to hardware primitives: `declare_intent` (fitness targets), `declare_ego` (state identity), `beacon` (kernel escalation), `strange_loop` (self-modifying computation), and `consciousness` (introspective processes).

We don't start with neural networks and hope structure emerges. We start with structure — the Fano plane, PSL(2,7), octonion algebra — and build systems that inherit mathematical guarantees.

Every state transition in EarthformOS is hash-chained. The kernel is auditable not by policy, but by architecture.
